## 3. Required Documents

### 3.1 Company Documents

Please prepare the following documents. All documents should be in English or Arabic; other languages require certified translation.

#### Core Documents (Required for All Clients)

| Document | Description | Format |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Certificate of Incorporation | Company registration document | PDF (certified copy) |
| Memorandum & Articles of Association | Company governing documents | PDF (certified copy) |
| Good Standing Certificate | Proof of active company status | PDF (recent, within 3 months) |
| Board Resolution | Authorization for Saudi expansion | PDF (signed, apostilled) |
| Shareholder Register | Current ownership structure | PDF |
| Financial Statements | Last 2 years audited financials | PDF |
| Company Profile | Business description and history | PDF or Word |

#### Shareholder/Director Documents

| Document | Description | Format |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Passport Copies | All shareholders and directors | PDF (color scan) |
| Proof of Address | Shareholders holding >25% | PDF (utility bill, bank statement) |
| CV/Bio | Key executives | PDF or Word |
| Authorization Letters | For designated representatives | PDF (signed, apostilled) |

#### Additional Documents (Based on Service Package)

| Package | Additional Documents |
|---------|---------------------|
| Silver+ | Business plan, revenue projections |
| Gold+ | Organizational chart, target job descriptions |
| Platinum | Strategic plan, marketing materials, product specs |

### 3.2 Document Requirements

**Authentication Requirements:**
- Notarization in country of origin
- Apostille (Hague Convention countries) OR
- Saudi Embassy attestation (non-Hague countries)
- Ministry of Foreign Affairs attestation (in Saudi Arabia)

**Document Checklist:**
```
□ Certificate of Incorporation (apostilled)
□ Memorandum & Articles of Association (apostilled)
□ Good Standing Certificate (apostilled)
□ Board Resolution (apostilled)
□ Shareholder Register
□ Financial Statements (2 years)
□ Passport copies (all shareholders/directors)
□ Proof of address (major shareholders)
□ Power of Attorney (apostilled)
□ Company Profile
```

### 3.3 Document Submission

**Submission Portal:**
Upload documents through your client portal at: portal.intersect-ksa.com

**Alternative Methods:**
- Email: documents@intersect-ksa.com
- Secure file transfer (for large files)

**File Naming Convention:**
`[CompanyName]_[DocumentType]_[Date].pdf`
Example: `AcmeCorp_CertificateOfIncorporation_20241125.pdf`

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How long does the full market entry process take?**
A: Typical timeline is 4-6 months, depending on sector complexity and document readiness.

**Q: Can I start operations before all licenses are obtained?**
A: No, commercial activities require all applicable licenses to be in place.

**Q: What if my documents are not in English or Arabic?**
A: We can assist with certified translation services (additional cost).

### Platform Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page or contact support.

**Q: Can I add more users to my account?**
A: Yes, admin users can add additional users through the platform settings.

**Q: Is my data secure on the platform?**
A: Yes, we use enterprise-grade security including encryption, access controls, and regular audits.

### Process Questions

**Q: What happens if my application is rejected?**
A: We'll analyze the rejection reasons and advise on remediation steps. Most rejections are due to documentation issues that can be resolved.

**Q: Can I change my service package during the project?**
A: Yes, packages can be upgraded. Please discuss with your Account Manager.

**Q: What support do I receive after the project completes?**
A: Post-project support options include retainer services and ongoing platform access subscriptions.
