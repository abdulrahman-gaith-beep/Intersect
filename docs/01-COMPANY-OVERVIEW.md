# Intersect - Company Overview

## About Intersect

**Intersect** is a premier soft landing company specializing in facilitating seamless market entry for international businesses into the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Leveraging cutting-edge AI technology and deep local expertise, we serve as the strategic bridge connecting global enterprises with the dynamic and rapidly growing Saudi market.

## Vision

To become the leading enabler of international business success in Saudi Arabia, powered by innovative AI solutions and unparalleled local market intelligence.

## Mission

To simplify and accelerate market entry for international companies into Saudi Arabia by providing comprehensive, AI-driven solutions that minimize risk, reduce time-to-market, and maximize success potential.
