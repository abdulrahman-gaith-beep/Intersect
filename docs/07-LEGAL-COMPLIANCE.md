## 2. Saudi Regulatory Framework Overview

### 2.1 Business Regulations

#### Companies Law (2015, Amended 2022)
- Governs all commercial entities
- Defines LLC, JSC, and other structures
- Sets corporate governance requirements
- Specifies shareholder rights and obligations

**Key Requirements:**
- Articles of Association registration
- Board meeting requirements
- Financial reporting obligations
- Audit requirements

#### Foreign Investment Law
- Administered by Ministry of Investment (MISA)
- Defines eligible activities for foreign investors
- Sets capital requirements
- Specifies ownership limitations (if any)

**Key Requirements:**
- Positive list of permitted activities
- Negative list of restricted activities
- Capital adequacy requirements
- Reporting obligations

### 2.2 Employment Regulations

#### Labor Law
- Governs employment relationships
- Sets working hours, leave, benefits
- Defines termination procedures
- Establishes dispute resolution

**Key Requirements:**
- Written employment contracts (Arabic)
- Maximum 48-hour work week
- 21-30 days annual leave
- End of service benefits
- Notice periods (30-90 days)

#### Saudization (Nitaqat)
- Mandates Saudi employment quotas
- Varies by sector and company size
- Enforced through visa system
- Regular reporting required

**Nitaqat Categories:**

| Category | Employment Ratio | Impact |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Platinum | >40% (varies) | Full visa services, expedited processing |
| Green (High) | 30-40% | Normal services |
| Green (Medium) | 20-30% | Normal services |
| Green (Low) | 10-20% | Limited services |
| Yellow | 5-10% | Restricted services |
| Red | <5% | No new visas, cannot transfer sponsorship |

#### Social Insurance (GOSI)
- Mandatory employee registration
- Contribution rates:
  - Employer: 12% (9% pension + 3% OSCH)
  - Employee: 10% (pension)
- Saudi and GCC employees only
- Monthly contribution deadline: 15th

### 2.3 Tax and Zakat

#### Value Added Tax (VAT)
- Rate: 15%
- Registration threshold: SAR 375,000
- Filing: Monthly (>SAR 40M) or Quarterly
- Record keeping: 6 years
- E-invoicing mandatory

**VAT-able Services:**
- Professional services
- Software and technology
- Consulting and advisory
- Training and education

**Zero-rated:**
- Exports
- International transportation
- Certain financial services

#### Corporate Income Tax
- Rate: 20% on foreign shareholder portion
- Applied on net taxable income
- Annual filing requirement
- Withholding tax on certain payments

#### Zakat
- Rate: 2.5% on Zakat base
- Applies to Saudi/GCC shareholder portion
- Annual filing requirement
- Zakat base = equity + provisions + loans - fixed assets

### 2.4 Data Protection

#### Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)
- Effective September 2023
- Regulates processing of personal data
- Establishes data subject rights
- Mandates data localization

**Key Requirements:**
- Consent for data processing
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization
- Security measures
- Breach notification
- Data subject access rights

#### Data Localization
- Saudi resident data must be stored locally
- Cross-border transfer restrictions
- Adequacy requirements for transfers
- NDMO guidance compliance

## 5. Data Protection Compliance

### 5.1 PDPL Compliance Program

**Data Processing Principles:**
1. Lawfulness, fairness, transparency
2. Purpose limitation
3. Data minimization
4. Accuracy
5. Storage limitation
6. Integrity and confidentiality
7. Accountability

### 5.2 Data Subject Rights

| Right | Description | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Access | Right to access personal data | 30 days |
| Rectification | Right to correct inaccurate data | 30 days |
| Erasure | Right to delete data | 30 days |
| Restriction | Right to limit processing | 30 days |
| Portability | Right to receive data | 30 days |
| Objection | Right to object to processing | 30 days |

### 5.3 Data Breach Response

**Breach Response Procedure:**
1. Detection and initial assessment (0-4 hours)
2. Containment and investigation (4-24 hours)
3. Authority notification (within 72 hours)
4. Data subject notification (without undue delay)
5. Remediation and review (ongoing)

### 5.4 Data Localization

**Requirements:**
- Saudi resident personal data stored locally
- Use of approved local cloud providers or data centers
- Cross-border transfer impact assessments
- Standard contractual clauses for transfers (where permitted)

## 8. Dispute Resolution

### 8.1 Saudi Courts System

**Court Hierarchy:**
1. Court of First Instance
2. Court of Appeal
3. Supreme Court

**Specialized Courts:**
- Commercial Court
- Labor Court
- Administrative Court
- Criminal Court

### 8.2 Alternative Dispute Resolution

**Arbitration:**
- Saudi Center for Commercial Arbitration (SCCA)
- UNCITRAL rules available
- Enforceable awards
- Neutral venue option

**Mediation:**
- Increasingly popular
- Court-annexed mediation
- Private mediation services

### 8.3 Recommended Approach

**For Commercial Disputes:**
1. Negotiation (30 days)
2. Mediation (30 days)
3. Arbitration (if unresolved)

**Arbitration Clause Template:**
*"Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this contract shall be finally settled under the Rules of the Saudi Center for Commercial Arbitration by one or more arbitrators appointed in accordance with said Rules."*
