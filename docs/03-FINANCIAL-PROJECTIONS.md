## 1. Revenue Projections

### 1.1 Revenue Model Overview

Intersect generates revenue through three primary channels:

1. **Project-Based Fees (60-65% of revenue)**
   - Market entry service packages
   - Custom consulting engagements
   - Add-on services

2. **Subscription Revenue (25-30% of revenue)**
   - AI platform access
   - Ongoing compliance monitoring
   - Market intelligence services

3. **Success Fees (5-10% of revenue)**
   - Partnership facilitation
   - Investment deal commissions

## 9. Financial Assumptions

### 9.1 Revenue Assumptions
- Average sales cycle: 3-4 months
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on completion
- Subscription churn: 15-20% annually
- Upsell rate: 25% of existing clients

### 9.2 Cost Assumptions
- Average salary (all employees): SAR 20,000/month + 20% benefits
- Salary increases: 5% annually
- Office rent: SAR 25,000/month (Year 1), scaling with headcount
- Marketing spend: 8-10% of revenue

### 9.3 Market Assumptions
- Saudi FDI continues to grow
- Vision 2030 programs remain active
- No major regulatory changes affecting business model
- Competition remains fragmented

### 9.4 Operational Assumptions
- Project completion: 90% on time
- Client satisfaction: 85%+
- Platform availability: 99.5%
- Team utilization: 70-80%
