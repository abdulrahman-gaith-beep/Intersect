# Intersect - Partnership Program

## Overview

Intersect's Partnership Program is designed to create mutually beneficial relationships with organizations that share our commitment to facilitating successful market entry for international companies into Saudi Arabia. This document outlines our partnership tiers, benefits, and engagement model.

---

## 1. Partnership Philosophy

### 1.1 Our Approach

At Intersect, we believe in building long-term, strategic partnerships that create value for all parties:
- Our clients benefit from expanded expertise and services
- Our partners benefit from referral revenue and market access
- Together, we deliver superior market entry experiences

### 1.2 Partnership Principles

**Mutual Value Creation**
Every partnership should benefit both parties through complementary strengths.

**Quality Over Quantity**
We prioritize deep, productive partnerships over a large number of shallow relationships.

**Transparency**
Clear communication about expectations, fees, and performance.

**Collaboration**
Joint efforts to serve clients and develop new opportunities.

**Integrity**
Ethical conduct and alignment with our values.

## 2. Partnership Tiers

### 2.1 Referral Partner

**Overview:**
Entry-level partnership for organizations that occasionally refer clients to Intersect.

**Ideal For:**
- Professional services firms (accounting, legal)
- Consultants with occasional Saudi market inquiries
- Business advisors and coaches
- Trade associations

**Benefits:**
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Referral Commission | 10% of first-year revenue |
| Partner Portal Access | Basic access |
| Marketing Materials | Co-brandable collateral |
| Partner Newsletter | Monthly updates |
| Training | Annual partner training session |

**Requirements:**
- Signed partnership agreement
- Minimum 2 referrals per year
- Compliance with referral process

**Commission Structure:**
- 10% of first-year client revenue
- Paid quarterly
- 6-month attribution window

### 2.2 Solution Partner

**Overview:**
For organizations that regularly collaborate with Intersect on joint client engagements.

**Ideal For:**
- Consulting firms with GCC practice
- Law firms with international trade focus
- Investment advisors
- Industry-specific consultancies

**Benefits:**
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Referral Commission | 15% of first-year revenue |
| Joint Marketing | Co-branded campaigns |
| Partner Portal Access | Full access |
| Training | Quarterly training sessions |
| Priority Support | Dedicated partner support |
| Event Participation | Speaking opportunities |
| Case Studies | Joint success stories |

**Requirements:**
- Signed partnership agreement
- Minimum 5 referrals per year
- Joint business planning
- Quality standards compliance

**Commission Structure:**
- 15% of first-year client revenue
- 5% of subsequent year revenue (2 years)
- Paid quarterly
- 12-month attribution window

### 2.3 Strategic Partner

**Overview:**
Deep integration partnership for organizations with significant Saudi market activity.

**Ideal For:**
- Major consulting firms
- Investment banks
- Trade promotion agencies
- Government investment bodies
- Chamber of commerce organizations

**Benefits:**
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Commission/Revenue Share | Custom (15-20%) |
| White-label Option | Branded platform access |
| Joint Go-to-Market | Coordinated campaigns |
| Executive Sponsorship | C-level relationship |
| Priority Access | New features and services |
| Strategic Planning | Annual business planning |
| Exclusive Territories | Industry or geographic exclusivity |
| Event Sponsorship | Joint events |

**Requirements:**
- Executive sponsorship
- Annual revenue commitment
- Joint business plan
- Dedicated partner manager
- Quality and brand standards compliance

**Commission Structure:**
- Custom negotiated (15-20%+)
- Revenue share options available
- Performance bonuses
- Multi-year attribution

### 2.4 Technology Partner

**Overview:**
For technology companies seeking integration with Intersect platform.

**Ideal For:**
- ERP/CRM providers
- HR/Payroll systems
- Document management platforms
- Compliance software vendors
- Translation services

**Benefits:**
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| API Access | Full API integration |
| Joint Development | Feature co-development |
| Marketplace Listing | Partner marketplace presence |
| Technical Support | Dedicated integration support |
| Co-marketing | Joint marketing activities |

**Requirements:**
- Technical integration
- Quality standards
- SLA commitments
- Security compliance

**Revenue Model:**
- Integration fees
- Usage-based revenue share
- Referral commissions

## 4. Partner Program Process

### 4.1 Becoming a Partner

**Step 1: Application**
- Complete partner application form
- Provide company information
- Describe partnership objectives

**Step 2: Evaluation**
- Review by Partnership Team
- Fit assessment
- Due diligence (Strategic partners)

**Step 3: Agreement**
- Partnership agreement negotiation
- Terms and conditions
- Contract signing

**Step 4: Onboarding**
- Portal access setup
- Training scheduling
- Introduction to team

**Step 5: Activation**
- Marketing material access
- Sales enablement
- First referral tracking

### 4.2 Referral Process

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    REFERRAL PROCESS                             │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                 │
│  Partner Identifies         Partner Registers        Intersect │
│  Opportunity          ─────▶ Lead in Portal    ─────▶ Qualifies│
│                                                       Lead     │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     ▼      │
│         │                         │              Intersect     │
│         │                         │              Engages       │
│         │                         │              Client        │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     ▼      │
│         │                         │              Contract      │
│         │                         │              Signed        │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     │      │
│         │                         │                     ▼      │
│         │                         │              Commission    │
│         │                         │              Paid          │
│         │                         │                            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Lead Registration Rules:**
- Register leads within 24 hours of identification
- Include client contact information
- Describe opportunity and timeline
- First to register earns attribution
- 6-12 month attribution window (by tier)

### 4.3 Joint Engagement Model

**For Solution and Strategic Partners:**

**Option 1: Referral Only**
- Partner refers client
- Intersect delivers all services
- Partner earns referral commission

**Option 2: Collaborative Delivery**
- Partner refers client
- Partner delivers complementary services
- Intersect delivers core services
- Revenue split as agreed

**Option 3: White-Label (Strategic Only)**
- Partner sells Intersect services as own
- Partner manages client relationship
- Intersect delivers services invisibly
- Premium revenue share
